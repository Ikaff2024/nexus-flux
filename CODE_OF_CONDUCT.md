# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We, the maintainers of **Nexus-Flux**, pledge to make participation in our project a harassment‑free, inclusive, and respectful experience for everyone — regardless of age, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, personal background, or technology choices.

Our goal is to build a community that reflects the spirit of innovation behind Nexus‑Flux.

---

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Demonstrating empathy and kindness
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing understanding when mistakes happen

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Harassment or discrimination of any kind
- Trolling, insults, or personal attacks
- Publishing private information without permission
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional environment

---

## Our Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Reviewing and addressing reported violations
- Taking fair, consistent corrective action when needed
- Removing contributions (code, issues, comments) that violate this Code

---

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to:
- The project repository
- Issue trackers
- Pull‑request discussions
- Project chat spaces and community channels
- Any public interaction involving Nexus‑Flux or its community

---

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported directly to our official security and ethics contact:

📩 **security@ikaffanan.com**

All reports will be reviewed promptly and confidentially.

---

## Enforcement Guidelines
Maintainers will take action appropriate to the severity of the offense, including:
1. **Correction** — Mild guidance where needed
2. **Warning** — Formal communication and expectations reset
3. **Temporary Ban** — For repeated or serious violations
4. **Permanent Ban** — For severe or repeated abusive behavior
